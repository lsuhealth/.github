# 🏥 About CALS

The **Center for Advanced Learning and Simulation (CALS)** at LSU Health New Orleans is a state-of-the-art healthcare education, training, and research center dedicated to advancing clinical practice through simulation-based learning and innovation.

CALS provides immersive, real-world training environments that replicate a wide range of healthcare settings using standardized patients, patient simulators, and fresh-tissue laboratories. Designed to support learners from students to experienced healthcare professionals, CALS emphasizes hands-on, competency-based education, reflective practice, and performance assessment. 

Learn more about us: https://www.medschool.lsuhsc.edu/medical_education/learningcenter/cals.aspx
