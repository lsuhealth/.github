# 🏥 About CALS

The **Center for Advanced Learning and Simulation (CALS)** at LSU Health New Orleans is a state-of-the-art healthcare education, training, and research center dedicated to advancing clinical practice through simulation-based learning and innovation.

CALS provides immersive, real-world training environments that replicate a wide range of healthcare settings using standardized patients, patient simulators, and fresh-tissue laboratories. The facility features multiple simulation suites, 16 standardized patient examination rooms, advanced audiovisual capture technology, and a 16-station cadaveric laboratory.

Designed to support learners from students to experienced healthcare professionals, CALS emphasizes hands-on, competency-based education, reflective practice, and performance assessment. In addition to training, CALS serves as a collaborative hub for healthcare technology development, simulation research, and interdisciplinary partnerships among academic institutions, healthcare organizations, and industry collaborators.
